Treatments per Drug Regimen

This graph shows how many treatments were given for each drug regimen. Each treatment has a maximum of ten time points when the drug was administered. However, not every subject completed all of the treatments. In the case of subject G989 there were duplicate time point entries, so the data for the entire subject was dropped from the analysis.
